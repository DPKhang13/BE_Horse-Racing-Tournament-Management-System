LUONG: Chu ngua dang ky giai va moi jockey

Muc dich luong:
Chu ngua dang nhap, chon race trong tournament, dang ky ngua tham gia race,
gui loi moi cho jockey, sau do jockey dang nhap va chap nhan loi moi.

Thu tu API:

1. POST /api/auth/login
   Muc dich: Chu ngua dang nhap de lay access token.

2. GET /api/tournaments/{tournamentId}/get-race-list
   Muc dich: Chu ngua xem danh sach race thuoc tournament de chon race dang ky.

3. GET /api/jockeys/get-all
   Muc dich: Chu ngua xem danh sach jockey kha dung de chon jockey moi tham gia.

4. POST /api/race-registrations/create
   Muc dich: Chu ngua dang ky ngua cua minh vao race.
   Ket qua can luu: registrationId/regId.

5. PUT /api/race-registrations/update/{registrationId}
   Muc dich: Chu ngua confirm phieu dang ky tham gia race.
   Trang thai confirm: ownerConfirmationStatus = confirmed.

6. PUT /api/race-registrations/approve/{registrationId}
   Muc dich: Admin hoac race referee duyet phieu dang ky race.
   Trang thai duyet: status = approved.

7. POST /api/jockey-assignments/create-invitation
   Muc dich: Chu ngua gui loi moi jockey cho registration vua tao.
   Ket qua can luu: assignmentId.

8. GET /api/jockey-assignments/get-sent-invitations
   Muc dich: Chu ngua kiem tra danh sach loi moi da gui.

9. POST /api/auth/login
   Muc dich: Jockey dang nhap de lay access token.

10. GET /api/jockey-assignments/get-my-invitations
   Muc dich: Jockey xem danh sach loi moi cua minh.

11. PUT /api/jockey-assignments/respond/{assignmentId}
   Muc dich: Jockey chap nhan hoac tu choi loi moi.
   Trang thai chap nhan: accepted.

12. GET /api/jockey-assignments/get-by-id/{assignmentId}
    Muc dich: Kiem tra lai loi moi sau khi jockey phan hoi.
